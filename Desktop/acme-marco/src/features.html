Track Changes

Suggest edits to content without making final changes. Edits are marked as suggestions & users can choose to accept or reject the changes.

Comments

Add, edit and delete comments on any content in the editor. Start discussions with comment threads, or give users comments-only access to your content.

Revision History

View, compare, restore or rename previous document versions.

Real-time Collaboration

Combine the Track Changes and Comments features with real-time collaborative editing, for a fully synchronous, GDocs-style editing experience.

Import from Word

Convert any number of Word documents into error-free HTML inside Acme. Styling is preserved, along with comments and suggestions from Track Changes.

Export to PDF and Word

Take your content anywhere with a single click. Maintain your formatting and structure.

AI Assistant

Add a customizable AI Assistant into your editor, ready to write, rephrase, translate or summarize your text

Spell and Grammar Check

Multilingual proofreading with autocomplete, custom dictionaries, and support for 80+ languages and dialects.

Pagination

See where the page breaks in your document are, ready for printing or exporting to other document formats.

Math Equations

Easily add properly formatted mathematical notation and chemical formulas to your content.
